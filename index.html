Research interest

  • •Theoretical spectroscopy, first-principles density functional theory 

  • •Model Hamiltonian, tight-binding method, Wannier function 

  • •Electronic structure of low dimensional systems, surfaces and interfaces, e.g., silicene, graphene-based nanostructures, MoS2, Fe-based superconductors 

  • •Electronic/thermoelectric transport of solid states 

  • •Spin-orbit coupling and Rashba effects 

Code developments

  • •KPROJ, unfolding electronic bands and simulating ARPES results of complex surfaces and interfaces based on layer k-projection method. 

  • •V2Boltz, vasp interface to BoltzTraP, calculating thermoelectric and electronic transport properties of materials.
